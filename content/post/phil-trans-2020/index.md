## Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society A

We are delighted to announce that the Special Issue of Philosophical Transactions A devoted to Ice Giant Systems has been published, and the full contents are described below.  On behalf of the Guest Editors of the issue and the journal team we would like to thank you very much for your input in shaping this issue.

Discussion meeting issue ‘Future exploration of ice giant systems’ organised and edited by Leigh N. Fletcher, Christopher S. Arridge, Athena Coustenis, Mark D. Hofstadter, Adam Masters and Amy A. Simon

Vol. 378, No. 2187, (December 2020) This issue is now available online at:
https://royalsocietypublishing.org/toc/rsta/2020/378/2187?ai=aqlb&ui=avn3&af=H
